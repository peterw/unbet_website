Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to Unbet, a mobile application designed to support individuals in their journey toward addiction recovery. At Unbet, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application and related services.

By using Unbet, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Account information (username, email address, password)
  • Profile information (age, gender, recovery goals, preferences)
  • Progress tracking data (sobriety milestones, check-ins, journal entries)
  • Communication data (messages with support groups, counselor interactions)
  • Device information (device type, operating system, unique device identifiers)

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect information about how you use our app:

  • App usage patterns and frequency
  • Features accessed and time spent in app
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Location data (only if explicitly permitted for meeting finder features)

2.3 Health and Recovery Data

With your explicit consent, we may collect:

  • Recovery progress information
  • Mood tracking data
  • Trigger identification information
  • Relapse prevention strategies
  • Anonymous survey responses for research purposes

HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our recovery support services
  • Personalize your recovery experience and recommendations
  • Track your progress and provide motivational content
  • Connect you with appropriate support resources
  • Improve our app functionality and user experience
  • Communicate important updates and recovery tips
  • Conduct research to improve addiction recovery methods (anonymized data only)
  • Ensure app security and prevent misuse

DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

4.1 Data Storage

  • Your data is stored on secure, encrypted servers located in the United States
  • We use industry-standard encryption both in transit and at rest
  • Sensitive health information is stored separately with additional security measures
  • Data backups are performed regularly and stored securely

4.2 Security Measures

  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Employee access controls and confidentiality agreements
  • Secure data transmission using SSL/TLS encryption
  • Regular software updates and security patches

4.3 Data Retention

  • Account data is retained while your account is active
  • Recovery progress data is retained for 7 years to support long-term recovery tracking
  • Communication data is retained for 2 years
  • Anonymized research data may be retained indefinitely
  • You may request data deletion at any time (subject to legal requirements)

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

5.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who may have access to your information:

  • Cloud hosting services (Amazon Web Services)
  • Analytics providers (Google Analytics for Firebase)
  • Customer support platforms (Zendesk)
  • Payment processors (Stripe) for premium features
  • Push notification services (Firebase Cloud Messaging)

5.2 Healthcare Partners

With your explicit consent, we may share relevant information with:

  • Licensed addiction counselors and therapists
  • Healthcare providers involved in your recovery
  • Emergency contacts in crisis situations
  • Insurance providers (only with explicit authorization)

5.3 Research Partners

We may share anonymized, aggregated data with:

  • Academic research institutions studying addiction recovery
  • Public health organizations
  • Non-profit addiction recovery organizations
  • Government health agencies (for public health research only)

5.4 Third-Party Commitments

All third-party partners are required to:

  • Maintain strict confidentiality agreements
  • Implement appropriate security measures
  • Use data only for specified purposes
  • Comply with applicable privacy laws
  • Notify us immediately of any data breaches

USER RIGHTS AND CHOICES

6.1 Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information stored in our systems
  • Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Download a copy of your data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

6.2 Privacy Settings

You can control:

  • Profile visibility to other users
  • Data sharing with healthcare providers
  • Location tracking permissions
  • Push notification preferences
  • Research participation consent
  • Third-party data sharing preferences

6.3 Communication Preferences

You may choose to:

  • Receive recovery tips and motivational messages
  • Get reminders for check-ins and milestones
  • Participate in community discussions
  • Receive updates about new features
  • Get emergency support notifications

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Unbet is not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

If you are located outside the United States, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

CHANGES TO THIS PRIVACY POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy in the app
  • Sending an email notification
  • Displaying a prominent notice in the app
  • Requiring acknowledgment of changes for continued use

LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal information based on:

  • Your consent for specific data uses
  • Contractual necessity to provide our services
  • Legitimate interests in improving addiction recovery support
  • Legal obligations under healthcare and privacy laws
  • Vital interests in emergency situations

YOUR CALIFORNIA PRIVACY RIGHTS

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date listed above and governs our collection, use, and disclosure of your information when using the Unbet mobile application.
